Medication Apps

Here are some popular medication apps and software that can help you manage your medications effectively

My Therapy

This app offers medication reminders, mood tracking, and health measurements like blood pressure and weight

MediSafe

A highly recommended app by physicians and pharmacists, Medisafe helps you track your medications, set reminders, and even share progress with friends or family

DoseCast

This app allows you to enter medication details and set reminders on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule

MedHelper
